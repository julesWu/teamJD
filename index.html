Our Story

Our story started in an unconventional way. To give you some context, Julia just moved to the Seattle area for her job, in which she was on Dawn's (David's mom) team. Dawn figured that because Julia and another intern on the team did not know anyone or the area very well, what better way for them to learn about it than from David (her son), who also just recently graduated.

Now, I know what you're thinking. She did this on purpose. She wanted to set them up. But that could not be further from the truth. Regardless of what her intentions were, when the day came for the three of them to meet, the intern bailed, so it was just Julia and David. Not wanting to be rude and cancel, Julia still met David at that Chipotle in Crossroads Bellevue. And what blossomed out of that first encounter was a surprise to them both: a friendship that neither parties expected.

As time grew, so did their knowledge of one another, and so did their affection for one another...which leads us to you...reading their story on their wedding website. I think things worked out pretty well for them - wouldn't you say?
